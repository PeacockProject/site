The Architecture

Four specialized tools. One unified experience.

Level 01

Peacock Builder

The core toolkit. It orchestrates the entire system architecture, allowing you to seamlessly compile and output flashable PeacockOS builds from source.

Level 02 : MTK

MinKernel

A scratch-built, incredibly lightweight chainloader explicitly tuned for MediaTek hardware, bridging the gap entirely where traditional bootloaders fall short. Inspired by the amazing work of lk2nd

BOOT BRIDGE
Level 02 : QCOM

lk2nd Fork

A highly customized boot bridge. It intercepts the standard boot sequence, injecting the exact modifications needed to successfully launch PeacockOS on your hardware, with the visual flare of course.

Level 03

PeacockOS

The operating system itself. A completely decentralized, unleashed environment designed from the ground up to give you absolute control over your device's capabilities. Elegant, fast, and engineered for those who demand more than factory limits.

Level 04

Peacock Recovery (PRP)

Engineered from the ground up as a native alternative to TWRP, designed exclusively to manage, rescue, and repair your PeacockOS environment.
